Double Filter Technology

2x the filters, 10x the speed. Relay's ground-breaking dual filter system is the secret to delivering effective water filtration that's as fast as your faucet.

Filter Once for Freshness

The Stage 1 Filter removes most contaminants from your tap water, leaving a small amount of chlorine to keep your water free from bacteria and other microbes if you plan on storing it in the fridge.

Filter Twice for Great Taste

Right before the water pours out of the spout, it travels through the Stage 2 Filter. The Stage 2 Filter removes any remaining chlorine, leaving you with nothing but pure, clean H20

Locking Lid Stays In Place

No more super-slowmo trips from the faucet to the fridge. The Relay's lid snapps on and locks tight, ensuring that you won't slosh water all over the kitchen floor.

No Spills When You Pour

CamelBak Relay filters at the speed of your faucet, which means it doesn’t need a filling reservoir (located on top of the most pitchers), making Relay easy to balance and virtually impossible to spill.
